Experiment Overview

I wanted to see how well it would perform at merging two realistic characters. Both girls where generated with Flux Max.

"Place both woman with red hairs from image 1 and the blonde woman from image 2 together in one desert scene where they are sitting toghther while maintaining the same facial features, hairstyle, clothes and expression"
