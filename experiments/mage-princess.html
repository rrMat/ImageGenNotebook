Mage & Princess Experiment

Guess what? You got it for free. Are you proud of yourself?

Experiment Overview

This experiment tries to merge two characters with different styles while retaining one of the two styles.

Prompt:

"A pixel art scene where the wizard meets the princess in a whimsical forest. Crucially, maintain the exact character likeness of both the wizard and the princess as provided in the reference images. The whimsical forest features glowing, oversized mushrooms, vibrant pixelated foliage, and ancient, gnarled trees with subtle, magical glow. Dappled sunlight filters through the canopy, creating soft, ethereal lighting on the forest floor. The overall style should evoke a charming and detailed 16-bit era video game aesthetic, with clear character expressions and a sense of wonder."

NAG Negative Prompt:

"noise, blurred, lowres"
